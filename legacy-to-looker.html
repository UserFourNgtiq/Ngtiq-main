Why look to Looker?

More and more enterprises have begun to fully leverage the cloud, making it the perfect place for serving big data analytics. Unlike on-prem, where storage and processing limitations prevent you from maximizing the value of data analytics, the cloud offers scalable compute resources and virtually limitless storage.

To resolve the storage, processing, and accessibility issues that weaken the on-prem model, look no further than Looker. Manual data analysis presents the risk of human error during data ingestion, which can skew results. Teams are hammered with requests for insights, creating an information bottleneck as they try to cater to the needs of all departments.

With Looker, the path from data to insights is short and fast. Business intelligence teams can enable stakeholders to do their own analysis in a governed environment, democratizing data and accelerating output velocity.

Breaking the barriers to information

The challenges of data resident in on-prem warehouses or platforms were detected by NGTIQ early on. To deliver industry-leading analytics services in the cloud, we decided to partner with Looker, a cloud-native data analytics and visualization platform.

Looker improves the visibility and accessibility of business insights and has been uniquely designed for use by non-technical audiences. LookML, a specialized in-house markup language, omits the need for writing manual queries, and provides a graphical user interface. By simplifying things, Looker resolves accessibility problems by allowing any user to generate meaningful business insights.

Legacy to Looker migration is efficient

Migrating from Legacy to Looker does not require development from scratch, as is the case with many modernization efforts. Looker enables reuse of most components that are developed in legacy data environments.
