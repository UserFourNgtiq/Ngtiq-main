The cloud has ushered in a new era of productivity and efficiency, enabling unprecedented performance and reliability for enterprise services while simultaneously reducing operating costs. While the hardware has improved significantly, optimized workflows are only beginning to evolve.

UiPath offers innovative robotic process automation (RPA) functionality for system processes and employee workflows.

UiPath Studio’s RPA functionality allows enterprises to automate their employee workflows, improving productivity and consistency. Within the UiPath dashboard, these workflows are visualized with flowcharts and sequencing charts. The dashboard dramatically simplifies analysis for IT departments, allowing them to decipher a workflow’s function and identify any dependencies to work optimally.

Trusted managers and team leaders can design their workflow automation tasks on UiPath, bypassing your development team and distributing process optimization across the business to reduce workloads.

NGTIQ UiPath Offerings

NGTIQ has partnered with UiPath to deliver RPA solutions to help our clients transform their business and grow with an automation-first mindset. Our partnership with UiPath enables us to unlock new productivity and process automation levels for our clients so they may modernize the workplace and workforce and elevate the customer experience.

We offer a full range of UiPath consulting services, including:

 

UiPath Implementation Services

UiPath is designed to integrate with your existing business systems and software to enable orchestration and automation via the platform. Using our cloud- and vendor-agnostic approach, NGTIQ experts will perform a full assessment of your hardware and software assets, creating a roadmap for integrating your existing business services with UiPath. Once UiPath is installed and your services are loaded into the platform, we can also create automated workflows and further configuration.

UiPath Workflow Optimization

UiPath allows you to visualize workflows and processes to generate insight on data flow and processing dependencies. With this insight, you can determine the inefficiencies of the workflows by referring to best practices for your chosen software package. By optimizing your workflows, you will reduce the processing requirements for each affected software package, with further performance improvements available via RPA. UiPath Workflow Optimization will minimize financial waste on processing and maximize workflow throughput.

UiPath ITSM and ITOM Automation

With UiPath, you can designate low-level IT administrative workloads to AI, which performs repeatable tasks with consistency, and significantly speed up task execution thanks to RPA. Your IT department can shift from “keeping the lights on” to developing and enhancing your infrastructure and services. The goal is to enable business growth rather than maintain business stasis. This applies to infrastructure management processes through ITOM and IT service management (ITSM) processes like software maintenance.

UiPath Process Discovery

An enterprise IT network functions mostly without IT engineers knowing what is happening in the background. UiPath offers task mining that can analyze system logs to identify hidden processes running on your infrastructure. These processes could be at the system or software-level; knowing that they exist can reveal new opportunities for automation and optimization. The discovery connects with the UiPath visualization function, allowing you to analyze and generate insight for these newly discovered processes to remediate bottlenecks or redundant processing overhead.

UiPath Workflow Automation Analytics

UiPath Insights acts as an analytics platform for your RPA workloads, allowing you to drill deep and monitor automated processes, ensuring they perform optimally. You can compile reports to communicate with business leaders and IT departments, detailing metrics like time savings, cost savings, and overall productivity of your process automation bots. In short, UiPath Insights gives you a complete picture of how the platform is performing against your business objectives, with visualized bot analytics that streamlines cost forecasting and KPI monitoring.
