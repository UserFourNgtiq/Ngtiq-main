NGTIQ Drupal Offerings

NGTIQ has partnered with Drupal to deliver innovative content management solutions to our clients. Our experts offer a wide range of services for the Drupal platform, including:

Drupal Implementation Services

For enterprises with an aging content management system (CMS), Drupal is an open digital experience platform to help you engage with customers. NGTIQ can help you adopt Drupal by converging your content assets within a single data warehouse. From here, content delivery pipelines are created to enable content streaming to your webpages with emphasis on high-availability and low-latency performance. We also cover content structure planning, user account creation, and role-based access control (RBAC) policy integration.

Drupal Custom Development

While Drupal benefits from a wealth of open-source add-ons provided by its user and developer community, it also offers extensive support for custom solutions. Our subject-matter experts can develop custom Drupal integrations, modules, and web pages that align with your marketing objectives. The custom development helps streamline your internal content workflows and processes while differentiating your enterprise from the competition with unique services and solutions. Drupal Custom Development applies to front-end user interface (UI) design, user experience (UX) design, and other aspects of web development, as well as back-end development of content pipelines, content storage systems, and more.

Drupal Intranet Provision

While Drupal is commonly used for customer-facing web pages, it can also be used for internal intranet services. A corporate intranet offers a centralized experience for internal employees, providing easy access to HR services, business web applications, and other collaborative platforms they need to use. By setting your intranet as the home page on employee devices, Drupal helps to promote teamwork and engagement, which ultimately increases productivity. In terms of content, Drupal intranet simplifies document- and file-sharing, with integrations across cloud storage platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP when using custom modules.

Drupal Desktop and Mobile Web Optimization

For existing websites, web page scripts and media assets can significantly increase latency, impacting the overall user experience. Drupal offers robust web page performance monitoring to help you identify bottlenecks caused by assets and scripts. Our experts approach Drupal optimization through database redevelopment, content delivery network (CDN) creation, user view, or page caching. Integrations with reverse proxies can also reduce malicious traffic to your site, and content asset compression in-transit can shave vital milliseconds off your page load times.

Drupal Multi-Lingual Enablement

Global businesses can capture the broadest possible audience with Drupal's multi-lingual functionality. For each piece of content in Drupal, you can apply seamless language translation based on assumed user locations. These locations are generated using a combination of IP addresses, browser settings, and ISP session IDs to detect and convert content into native languages accurately. Drupal offers support for more than 100+ languages with multiple dialects and right-to-left webpage conversion to accommodate any visitor to your site automatically.
