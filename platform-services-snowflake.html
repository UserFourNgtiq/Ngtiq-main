The NGTIQ experts can help you migrate from these legacy platforms to Snowflake to achieve better performance and reliability, and to scale operations in the cloud.

Snowflake Data Lake Implementation Services

A data lake acts as a central repository for your raw datasets. Snowflake is an excellent data lake hosting platform, as you only pay for the storage and compute resources you use. Cloud Data Analytics

This allows the NGTIQ experts to build secure pipelines between analytics platforms and Snowflake, with scalable compute and a wide range of supported data types.
