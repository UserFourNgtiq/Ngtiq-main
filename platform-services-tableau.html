NGTIQ Tableau Offerings

Tableau delivers simplified data analytics, and NGTIQ provides the technology foundation for Tableau to run optimally. Our experts can step in to assess your existing infrastructure and create an adoption roadmap to keep you informed at every stage of the project.

 

Tableau Implementation Planning

NGTIQ’s certified Tableau consultants have the required expertise to plan and implement the Tableau platform. Our experts will assess your existing IT infrastructure to identify compatibility issues and offer solutions from our partners to expedite the adoption of Tableau. We then execute by licensing and installing Tableau, configuring the platform for your business needs, and connecting data sources. From here, we can offer support and guidance to maximize the returns from Tableau, using integrations, visualizations, and pipeline optimization.

Data Discovery and Visualizations

Tableau requires data to function, and the NGTIQ experts can create pipelines that extract and load data to the platform. This data can come from any cloud platform or database dialect, thanks to Tableau’s platform-agnostic functionality. From here, our experts can identify and propose suitable visualizations from the Tableau public gallery or design custom visualizations around your specific business needs. These visualizations are then integrated into your employees’ Tableau dashboards with data customization based on seniority or department for granular compliance and security controls.

Self-Service Analytics

Tableau can integrate with role-based access control (RBAC) and identity access management (IAM) policies on your cloud hosting platform. This enables self-service analytics that allow users to generate their insights without compromising compliance or security. By empowering your users to perform their analytics, you promote a data-driven work culture. NGTIQ can help you configure your cloud platform and Tableau to deliver self-service analytics, resulting in enhanced productivity and better decision making.

Tableau Connector Integration

Tableau offers a broad range of connectors to integrate your hosting platforms and digital software packages. This includes support for AWS, Azure, Snowflake, and Google Cloud for database loading and extraction. Tableau Connectors are also dialect-agnostic, allowing you to integrate MariaDB, MongoDB, OracleDB, PostgreSQL, MySQL, MSSQL, or any other mainstream database system with the Tableau analytics platform. You can continue using your preferred and established technologies with Tableau fully accommodating multi-vendor systems and software.

Tableau Data Quality Management

Data quality and insight quality are inextricably linked, making this an important issue. NGTIQ can design ETL pipelines that cleanse your data before loading into Tableau, removing, and de-duplicating redundant data to maximize the quality of insights on the platform. Our experts leverage data quality warnings on Tableau to establish a minimum quality threshold and ensure consistent, high-quality insights, even when loading raw and unstructured data from a data lake.
