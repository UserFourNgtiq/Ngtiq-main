Automation Anywhere Compliance Management

Data protection regulations and laws must be adhered to by enterprises. To simplify this, Automation Anywhere offers compliance auditing and remediation using RPA. The bots are injected into your DevSecOps CI/CD pipelines, where application updates can be automatically vetted for security and compliance issues. The bot will assess the operational status of software on your network, comparing the real-time state to your role-based access control (RBAC) policies. When deviations occur, the bot will either inform your IT department or remediate it with the correct RPA framework. The audit and remediation both serve to improve your mean time to acknowledge (MTTA) and reduce your mean time to remediation (MTTR) for compliance and security problems, to ensure full contractual and regulatory adherence.

Automation Anywhere Data Ingestion and Loading

Automation Anywhere bots also include data management. The bots allow you to automate extract-transform-load (ETL) processes on your network, enabling the seamless transfer of data between your data warehouse and software platforms. With ETL automation on Automation Anywhere, your enterprise can leverage live data for real-time analytics and business intelligence processing. The BI dramatically improves your decision-making agility and allows stakeholders to quickly identify and remediate department-level problems before they impact your broader operations.
