E-waste Management Initiative

Electronic waste or e-waste contains many valuable materials that can be used to produce new electronic equipment such as aluminum, copper, gold, silver, plastics, and ferrous metals. However, it also contains several hazardous materials like mercury, lead, cadmium, beryllium, chromium, and chemical flame retardants. In order to conserve natural resources from this electronic waste while getting rid of the toxic materials, Ngtiq, in an attempt to save the environment, has tied up with the Ms/ Vardhman Sales Agency for the collection and disposal of all the e-waste on PAN India level. The collected items are then channelized to our e-waste partner plant for final processing.
