Our Approach

We start by performing an assessment of your current state. We look at your data flows, sources, storage, business consumption, and responsibilities—and we conduct data profiling, as well as a quality assessment and documentation process.

Leveraging what we have learned from the current state analysis, we design and implement a data governance framework. This is followed by data cleansing and enrichment.

The next step is to pre-load regulatory driven glossaries mapped to data controls and process inventories. We also document and automate policies and procedures. Then we establish a comprehensive framework for security, data privacy, and compliance with regulatory norms.

At this point, we redesign a future-ready data and technical architecture. Finally, we migrate code and data to the hosted location for ongoing services, enhancements, and support.

With Big Data comes, big responsibilities and challenges too! The fact of the matter is that a solid data architecture foundation, management, and governance is key to realized targeted outcomes and business success. It’s an investment worth making to not only ensure data quality and reliability, but meeting compliance requirements as well as improving decision making.
