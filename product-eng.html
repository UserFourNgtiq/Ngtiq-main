Amazon acquires Athena Federated Query IP from NGTIQ

Time to market is everything in digital transformations. In order to accelerate the delivery of Analytics, NGTIQ took a pioneering approach. We decided to decouple migration or physical unification of data from the main goal of building analytics. Therefore, instead of taking data to the cloud- we decided to bring the cloud to data.

NGTIQ connected Amazon's powerful federated data querying platform- Athena with data sources anywhere on the planet. We built custom connectors from AWS to databases such as Oracle, Terradata, SAP Hana, Mainframe DB2, Sybase and many more. NGTIQ Athena connectors are able to query millions of rows of data from disparate sources and join them on the Athena platform for generating powerful insights.

In the first quarter of 2022, NGTIQ announced the acquisition of this IP by AWS. Since their launch in April 2022, over 700 AWS customers have deployed these connectors and have accelerated their analytics life cycles.
