NGTIQ Talend Offerings

With Talend, NGTIQ can facilitate modernized data ingestion across on-prem and cloud infrastructure deployments. This implementation will improve the quality and reliability of your data while generating insight, allowing your business to make better decisions.

 

Talend End-to-End ETL

The process of migrating data from a source to a destination is tedious and complicated. Legacy systems require conversion to work with modern database solutions, and digital services need pipelines to facilitate data transfer to and from the database. NGTIQ can deliver end-to-end ETL services using Talend. We focus on eliminating data siloes by centralizing your data stores. This leads to simplified compliance and security, along with easier data warehouse troubleshooting and improved query performance.

Talend Data Lakes

Data lakes are repositories for your raw data that separates low-quality raw data from high-quality insight data. Information in a data lake is associated with identifiers and tags for faster query results while incurring a minimal cost through hosting on lower cloud performance tiers. NGTIQ can provide a data lake through Talend on major cloud platforms, with support across Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Snowflake.

Talend Master Data Management (MDM)

Talend MDM provides a real-time overview of your data sources and any connections from third-party services. You can monitor data flowing through pipelines, track network utilization on your LAN or cloud network, and view dataset history records to gain a holistic understanding of your data warehouse. NGTIQ leverages Talend MDM to improve enterprise data agility by increasing visibility and reducing decision-making latency, thanks to real-time monitoring.
