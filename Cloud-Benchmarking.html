What is Cloud Benchmarking?

Cloud benchmarking is a multi-faceted procedure that provides an independent perspective to help organizations understand where their cloud capabilities lie on the digital maturity curve, both in absolute and competitive terms.

The insights are based on benchmarking the client’s cloud capabilities against peers and the best in class across industries. This helps organizations learn where gaps in their cloud environment are vis-a-vis the competition, so they know where to invest their time, effort, and resources to maximize the value and performance of their cloud initiatives.
