NGTIQ Microsoft PowerBI Offerings

NGTIQ is a Microsoft Gold Partner with competencies across Azure implementation and maintenance, as well as the PowerBI analytics platform. Our experts are highly skilled in management of hardware and software on your Microsoft-based network through the following services:

 

Microsoft PowerBI Implementation

Though PowerBI is a powerful software, it comes with its own set of complexities. Our experts can help you move to the Azure cloud platform to leverage the benefits of PowerBI fully. From here, we can link your data sources via PowerBI Access Integrations using the REST-API, offering support for Microsoft, AWS, Google Cloud, and Snowflake database repositories, if preferred. NGTIQ will determine your business requirements and build a solution scope for PowerBI before loading data to the platform. From here, we can develop reporting and querying processes for employee use and provide any necessary training.

PowerBI Dashboards and Visualizations

PowerBI is a visual analytics platform, and dashboards are the delivery mechanism. We can create customized dashboards for specific departments or design specialized executive dashboards to help with decision-making processes. These dashboards adhere to data security and compliance policies using role-based access controls (RBAC), giving authorized employees access to data without overprovisioning. The experts at NGTIQ will also design visualizations that can be saved and reused indefinitely, offering employees a quick and easy way to generate insights.

PowerBI Self-Service Reporting

Microsoft PowerBI gives your employees access to real-time analytics via self-service. This means employees can generate reports and execute queries with authorized datasets on demand, negating the need for traditional data analysts. NGTIQ will help you design self-service dashboards for use across the business, resulting in wider adoption of, and engagement in, data-driven analytics. This will promote a data-driven work culture founded on statistical evidence, empowering your employees to make smarter decisions in all business areas.

PowerBI Data Warehousing

A good data warehouse needs to ingest and process data efficiently while upholding data validity and integrity, all without breaking the bank. NGTIQ can design and implement a data warehouse solution optimized for PowerBI, with scalable storage and compute nodes that adapt to fluctuating processing demands. For this, we can use the Microsoft Azure Data Warehouse platform, Amazon Redshift, Google BigQuery, or the Snowflake Data Warehouse, depending on your business requirements and preferences. Then, we will implement robust, performance-driven data pipelines to feed PowerBI with data, enabling real-time analytics and BI functionality.

PowerBI Data Modelling

Raw data needs to be structured before being used in analytics or BI software. NGTIQ offers PowerBI data modeling to identify and transform raw datasets using Microsoft PowerPivot. This converts data for use by PowerBI by creating calculated tables and columns and the tagging of time-based data points to maximize data potential during analysis. Our experts can create the workflows and processes you need to continually model your data, ensuring PowerBI is always fed real-time information.
