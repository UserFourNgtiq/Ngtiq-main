Managed Replication

While planning a disaster recovery strategy, the business must adhere to the 3-2-1 approach. 1 - backup stored off-site, 2 – separate type of storage mediums with the other backups. 3 - Replication is the final aspect of 3-2-1 with three backups of your data.

Ngt-iq can configure network for data replication, and spread data backups across three separate locations in line with the approach. Data replication is an continuing process, which is why it is included with the DRaaS managed service. Our DRaaS services function around a data framework, giving immediate access to enterprise-grade resiliency and virtually zero RPO replication in the cloud.
