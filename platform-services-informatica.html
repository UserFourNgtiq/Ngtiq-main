NGTIQ Informatica Offerings

By partnering with Informatica, NGTIQ offers numerous consulting services to our clients, including:

 

Informatica Implementation Services

NGTIQ offers end-to-end Informatica implementation services, starting from planning and assessment to completing the installation. Throughout this process, we assess the candidacy of your existing infrastructure and software compatibility before recommending alternative solutions from our partners when needed. With the right foundation in place, we then install Informatica and configure your data connections.

AI-Powered Data Source Discovery

Informatica’s AI functionality allows us to drill deep into your infrastructure and identify data sources. This provides a unified view of your data infrastructure with monitoring and insight into the health and performance of your chosen data-hosting vendors. From here, you can orchestrate your data, view the data pipeline activity, and manage governance policies from a centralized interface.

Informatica Master Data Management

A master data source offers a single source of the truth (SSOT) for your enterprise. This combines multiple data sources, removing duplicates and connecting data points for a single reference point when generating insights. Informatica’s Master Data Management (MDM) functionality allows NGTIQ to deliver holistic customer, product, supplier, and inventory data to clients. This improves the consistency and accuracy of your data, leading to better insights and smarter business decisions.

Informatica Data Integration

Many enterprises have IT data siloes that store information in different formats across different platforms, thus reducing data mobility and limiting the amount of insights you can generate. NGTIQ offers data integration on Informatica to break down siloes and centralize your datasets. This allows multi-cloud databases to converge and digital software to connect seamlessly with your data for an interlinked ecosystem. NGTIQ integration results in better data usage and insight generation, leading to agile decision making and complete data visibility.

Informatica Data Quality

When you give consumers input forms, there will inevitably be duplicate or redundant entries into your database. This can impact the quality of your data, resulting in misleading insights. The same can happen with manual data entry by employees. NGTIQ offers Informatica data quality services that analyze your databases and identify redundant information. We also monitor and filter your data pipelines to reduce the quantity of bad data loaded into Informatica. The result is improved data quality and reduced storage overhead in extreme cases, leading to better performance and more valuable insights.

Informatica ETL Automation

Informatica offers AI-powered functionality through its CLAIRE assistant, which uses metadata to automate data orchestration and pipelines, and provide process optimization. It also includes the automatic creation of data relationships, helping your data stewards by automating statistical inference generation. NGTIQ can help you configure CLAIRE to suit your business needs, bringing self service capabilities to your data management workflows while empowering your workforce to become more agile and drive innovation.
